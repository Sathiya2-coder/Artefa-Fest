Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the events? +

All students and professionals are welcome to participate in ARTIFA FEST. There are both technical and non-technical events catering to different skill levels and interests.

How do I register for events? +

Navigate to the Registration page, fill out the form with your details, and choose the events you wish to participate in. You'll receive a confirmation email once registered.

Are there team events? What's the team size? +

Yes! Many of our events support team participation. Team sizes vary by event - some require 2 members, while others allow up to 4 members. Check the event details for specific team size requirements.

What are the prizes? +

We offer prizes totaling over ₹100,000 across all events! Winners will receive cash prizes, certificates, and recognition. Prize amounts vary depending on the event category and difficulty level.

When and where will the events take place? +

Check the Schedule section above for the complete timeline of all events. All events will take place at the Main Venue. Exact dates and times are listed in the schedule with venue details.

Can I participate in multiple events? +

Yes! You can participate in multiple events as long as there are no scheduling conflicts. Register for all the events you're interested in on the registration page.
