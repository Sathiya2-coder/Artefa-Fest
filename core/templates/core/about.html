What is ARTIFA FEST?

ARTIFA FEST is not just another symposium—it's an interdimensional experience where the mysterious "Upside Down" of Hawkins meets the cutting-edge world of Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. Organized by the Department of AI & DS, this premier event serves as a portal between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Imagine a world where Demogorgons aren't just monsters, but complex algorithms waiting to be tamed. Where the Mind Flayer isn't just a villain, but a network of data waiting to be understood. That's ARTIFA FEST—a celebration of innovation, creativity, and the brave souls who dare to explore beyond the ordinary.

Our Mission

To create an interdimensional platform where students can transcend conventional boundaries and demonstrate their technical prowess, creativity, and collaborative spirit. We're not just hosting competitions—we're opening portals to innovation in AI and Data Science.

  • Foster interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Bridge theory with practical application
  • Create memorable learning experiences
  • Inspire the next generation of innovators

Our Vision

To establish ARTIFA FEST as the definitive interdimensional symposium—a beacon of innovation that attracts the brightest minds from across multiple "dimensions" of knowledge. We envision a future where every participant leaves not just as a competitor, but as an explorer who has journeyed through the Upside Down of technology.

  • Lead regional AI & DS innovation
  • Create lasting industry connections
  • Build a community of innovators
  • Set new standards for tech events

Our Journey Through Time

2023

The First Portal

ARTIFA FEST was born as a small departmental event with just 5 technical events and 100 participants. The foundation of our interdimensional journey was laid.

2024

Expanding Dimensions

We introduced non-technical events and workshops, doubling our participant count. The Stranger Things theme was introduced, creating our unique identity.

2025

Interdepartmental Reach

ARTIFA FEST became an interdepartmental symposium with participants from across campus. We introduced hackathons and AI workshops.

2026

The Ultimate Experience

This year marks our biggest event yet with 20+ events, industry collaborations, and an enhanced Stranger Things theme. The Upside Down has never been this accessible.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We believe in pushing boundaries and exploring uncharted territories in AI and Data Science. Every event is designed to spark creative thinking.

Collaboration

The Upside Down teaches us that no one faces challenges alone. We foster teamwork and collective problem-solving across all events.

Learning

Every competition, workshop, and interaction is an opportunity to learn and grow. We prioritize skill development over just winning.
